# Problem

## Core Problem

Hiring and job search workflows rely too heavily on resumes, keywords, and blunt filters, which miss team fit and decision-making style.

## Who Feels It

- Candidates looking for environments that match how they actually work
- Small and mid-size teams that care about pace, mission, autonomy, and collaboration fit

## Why It Matters Now

Teams want better signal earlier in hiring, and candidates increasingly want fit clarity before committing.
